Every module is typed, versioned, auditable, and fully accessible programmatically.

---

## 🚀 Core Philosophy

- **Engagement** — audience interaction, campaigns, and creator–fan mechanics  
- **Contests** — giveaways, challenges, and automated competition workflows  
- **Resources** — unified asset, file, and document management  
- **Security** — permissions, access control, and operational safety  
- **Insurance** — risk scoring, liability modeling, and protection modules  
- **Updates** — versioning, patching, and system‑wide change propagation  
- **AI** — native AI integration across all services  
- **Autopilot** — automated workflows, triggers, and background processes  

Everything in InfluenceOS is accessible **programmatically** through modular, typed, versioned services.

## 📦 Architecture Overview

InfluenceOS is built around **isolated micro‑modules**, each with:

- Independent versioning  
- Strong typing  
- Audit logging  
- Risk scoring  
- API‑first design  
- Configurable workflows  
- Universal linking and traceability  

This ensures the system remains scalable, maintainable, and enterprise‑ready.
